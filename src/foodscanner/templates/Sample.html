Pregnancy Nutrition Guide

Essential nutrition guidance for your pregnancy journey

Essential Nutrients

  • Folic Acid: 600-800 mcg daily
  • Iron: 27 mg daily
  • Calcium: 1000 mg daily
  • Vitamin D: 600 IU daily
  • Omega-3: 200-300 mg DHA daily
  • Vitamin B12: 2.6 mcg daily

Recommended Foods

  • Leafy greens for folate
  • Lean proteins for development
  • Whole grains for energy
  • Fatty fish for brain development
  • Greek yogurt for calcium
  • Eggs for choline

Health Tips

  • Stay hydrated with 8-10 glasses of water
  • Eat small, frequent meals
  • Include fiber-rich foods
  • Avoid raw or undercooked foods
  • Limit caffeine intake
  • Choose nutrient-dense snacks
